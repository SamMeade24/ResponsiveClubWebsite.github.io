About the Church

St. Michael's is an Anglican church in the Catholic tradition.

Back in 1905, fundraising began for building the church, with the foundation stone being laid in 1907.

120 years later, this beautiful church in the heart of South East London is still serving the local community.

With a selection of masses led throughout the week by Father David Sherratt, please join us for prayer and reflection.
